Membership


Membership Dues

If you are unsure about joining, your first two practices are free to attend! After that, membership dues are $30/term or $70/year to continute partcipating the in the club. Dues help pay for replacing and maintaining equipment, renting shooting space, hosting events, and attending tournaments. You can pay your dues at club practices (with cash or check) or online on OSUSLI.


USA Archery Member Discount

If you are a USA Archery member, you only need to pay $30 a year for your club dues (instead of $70).
