Membership


Membership Dues

If you are unsure about joining, your first two practices are free to attend! After that, membership dues are $30/term or $70/year to continute partcipating the in the club. Dues help pay for replacing and maintaining equipment, renting shooting space, hosting events, and attending tournaments.

You can pay your dues at club practices (with cash or check) or online through OSU SLI. to pay member dues on SLI, open your home page and click "Membership details" under "Current Memberships" (see image). Then, you will see "Pay Member Dues" under Archery Club.


USA Archery Member Discount

If you are a USA Archery member, you only need to pay $30 a year for your club dues (instead of $70).

Questions? Email us at archery@oregonstate.edu or ask in our Discord server!
