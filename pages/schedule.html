Practice Schedule


Spring Term Practice Schedule: (last updated 4/1/2025)

  • Archery Club practices every week on Sundays and Tuesdays.
  • Tuesday practices are at EE Wilson from 6:00 to 7:30 pm. The campus carpool meets at 5:25 pm.
  • Sunday practices are at the OSU Equestrian Center Horse Arena from 11:00 am to 1:00 pm. The campus carpool meets at 10:45 am.
  • Our on-campus carpool meeting spot is near Dryden Hall (see the locations section for more details). If you are willing to help carpool please indicate how many seats you have available on the sign up sheet and bring your car to the meeting spot. You are also welcome to drive yourself or bike to the range.
  • For the most specific and up-to-date times and meeting details, we recommend joining the Archery Club Discord or signing up for our mailing list by becoming a member.
