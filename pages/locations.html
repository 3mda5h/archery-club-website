Locations

EE Wilson Archery Range

The EE Wilson Archery Range is located 10 miles north of Corvallis. This range is owned and operated by the Oregon Department of Fish and Wildlife and is open to the public. It has a maximum distance of 100 yards. The club meets on-campus to carpool to this range. (See "On-campus meeting spot" below).

OSU Equestrian Center

The OSU Equestrian Center Horse Arena is located about two miles west of campus and offers a maximum shooting distance of 20 yards. Please note that unlike EE Wilson, this space is only available to shoot at during official Archery Club practices.

Our club meets on-campus to carpool to this range (See "On campus meeting spot" tab). Note that this range is also within biking distance and accessible via the Campus Way and Walnut Blvd bike paths.
