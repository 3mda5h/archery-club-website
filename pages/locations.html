Locations


EE Wilson Archery Range

The EE Wilson Archery Range is an outdoor range located about 10 miles north of Corvallis. This range is owned and operated by the Oregon Department of Fish and Wildlife and is open to the public. It has a maximum shooting distance of 100 yards. Depending on the time of year, the range may be muddy and/or cold, so please dress accordingly.

The club meets on-campus at Dryden hall about 30 min before practice to carpool to this range. (See "On-campus meeting spot" below).

OSU Equestrian Center Horse Arena

The OSU Equestrian Center Horse Arena is located about two miles west of campus and offers a maximum shooting distance of 20 yards. Please note that unlike EE Wilson, this location is only available to shoot at during official Archery Club practices. Additionally, it is extemely important that participants do not leave behind any arrows or other objects on the floor of the arena, as they are a danger to the horses that use the space.

Our club meets on-campus at Dryden hall to carpool to this range (See "On campus meeting spot" below). Note that this range is also within biking distance and accessible via the Campus Way and Walnut Blvd bike paths.

On Campus Carpool Meeting Spot - Dryden Hall

If you need a ride to the range or are driving other people to the range, meet us on the sidewalk near Dryden hall, as shown in the image above. The club also meets here to carpool to tournaments or other events. Dryden hall is on the west side of campus near the intersection of 30th and Washington Way.
